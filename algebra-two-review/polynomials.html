Adding and Subtracting Polynomials

This process is actually simple

  • Step 1: Distrubute the signs
  • Step 2: Rewrite Like Terms next to each other
  • Step 3: Combine like Terms

Example problems (adding)

(3x^3 + 2x^2 - x + 7) + (x^3 - 10x^2 + 8)

Step 1: Distrubute the signs:(since anything times by a postive it their current sign all the signs stay the same)

3x^3 + 2x^2 - x + 7 + x^3 - 10x + 8

Step 2: Rewrite Like Terms next to each other

3x^3 + x^3 + 2x^2 - 10x^2 - x - 7 + 8

Step 3: Combine like Terms

4x^3 - 8x^2 - x + 1

(12x^5 - 3x^4 + 2x - 5) + (8x^4 - 3x^3 + 4x + 1)

Step 1: Distrubute the signs:(since anything times by a postive it their current sign all the signs stay the same)

12x^5 - 3x^4 + 8x^2 + 2x + 4x + 1

Step 2: Rewrite Like Terms next to each other

12x^5 - 3x^3 + x^3 + 2x^2 - 10x^2 - x - 7 + 8

Step 3: Combine like Terms

12x^5 + 5x^4 - 3x^3 + 6x - 4

Example problems (subtracting)

(3x^2 + 2x^2 - x - 7) - (x^3 - 10x^2 + 8)

Step 1: Distrubute the signs:(remember when you times something by a negitve it comes the opposite of their current sign.)

3x^2 - 2x^2 + x - 7 + -x^3 +10x^2 +8

Step 2: Rewrite Like Terms next to each other

3x^3 - x^3 + 2x^2 + 10x^2 - x - 7 - 8

Step 3: Combine like Terms

2x^3 + 12x^2 - x - 15
