Adding and Subtracting Polynomials

This process is actually simple

  • Step 1: Distrubute the signs
  • Step 2: Rewrite Like Terms next to each other
  • Step 3: Combine like Terms

Example problems (adding)

(3x^3 + 2x^2 - x + 7) + (x^3 - 10x^2 + 8)

Step 1: Distrubute the signs:(since anything times by a postive it their current sign all the signs stay the same)

3x^3 + 2x^2 - x + 7 + x^3 - 10x + 8

Step 2: Rewrite Like Terms next to each other

3x^3 + x^3 + 2x^2 - 10x^2 - x - 7 + 8

Step 3: Combine like Terms

4x^3 - 8x^2 - x + 1



(12x^5 - 3x^4 + 2x - 5) + (8x^4 - 3x^3 + 4x + 1)

Step 1: Distrubute the signs:(since anything times by a postive it their current sign all the signs stay the same)

12x^5 - 3x^4 + 8x^2 + 2x + 4x + 1

Step 2: Rewrite Like Terms next to each other

12x^5 - 3x^3 + x^3 + 2x^2 - 10x^2 - x - 7 + 8

Step 3: Combine like Terms

12x^5 + 5x^4 - 3x^3 + 6x - 4

Example problems (subtracting)

(3x^2 + 2x^2 - x - 7) - (x^3 - 10x^2 + 8)

Step 1: Distrubute the signs:(remember when you times something by a negitve it comes the opposite of their current sign.)

3x^2 - 2x^2 + x - 7 + -x^3 +10x^2 +8

Step 2: Rewrite Like Terms next to each other

3x^3 - x^3 + 2x^2 + 10x^2 - x - 7 - 8

Step 3: Combine like Terms

2x^3 + 12x^2 - x - 15



(-4x^2 - 2x - 6) - (-7x^2 - 2x -2)

Step 1: Distrubute the signs:(remember when you times something by a negitve it comes the opposite of their current sign.)

-4x^2 - 2x - 6 + 7x^2 -2x +2

Step 2: Rewrite Like Terms next to each other

-4x^2 + 7x^2 - 2x + 2x - 6 + 2

Step 3: Combine like Terms

3x^2 -4

Example problems (Multiplying)

(2x - 2)(3x + 5)

Step 1: Do FOIL(First Outside Inside Last)

First: 2x * 3x = 6x^2

Outside:2x * 5 = 10x

Inside: -2 * 3x = -6x

Last: -2 * 5 = -10

Step 2: Combine like terms

10x - 6x = 4x

Answer: 6x^2 + 4x - 10



(3x - 3)(x - 4)

Step 1: Do FOIL(First Outside Inside Last)

First: 3x * x = 3x^2

Outside:3x * -4 = -12x

Inside: -3 * x = -3x

Last: -3 * -4 = 12

Step 2: Combine like terms

-12x - 3x (Which also equals: -12 + -3x (Keep, Change, Change)) = -15x

Answer: 3x^2 - 15x + 12

Example problems (Dividing)

(m^3 - 6m^2 - 16m + 63) / (m-7)

Step 1: find the quotient of the first term in the dividend and the varible in the divisior

m^3 / m = m^2 (the trick when dividing with exponents is to just minus) m as an exponent of one so 3-1 is two which is how we got m^2

In turn m^2 is the first term of our answer

Step 2: multiply the answer you got in the first step with number in the divisor

m^2 * -7 = 7m^2

step 3: subtract the answer you got in the previous step from the second term in the dividend

-6m^2 - (-7m^2) (or -6m^2 + + 7m^2) = m^2

Step 4: divide the pervious answer by the varible in the divisor

m^2 / m = m

m is the second term in our answer. (we currently got m^2 +m)

Step 5: multiply the previous answer by number in the dividend

m * -7 = -7m

step 6: subtract the answer you got in the previous step from the third term in the dividend

-16m - (-7) (or -16m + + 7) = -9m

Step 7: divide the pervious answer by the varible in the divisor

-9m / m = -9

Inturn -9 is the third term in our annswer

Step 8: multiply the previous answer by number in the dividend

-9 * -7 = 63

step 6: subtract the answer you got in the previous step from the forth term in the dividend

63 - 63 = 0

This means our full answer is m^2 + m - 9
